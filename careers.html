Pharmaceuticals Marketing Specialist

We are looking for a passionate and results-oriented Pharmaceutical Marketing Specialist to join our team!

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute strategic marketing plans for assigned pharmaceutical products.
  • Conduct thorough market research to identify trends, competitor analysis, and customer needs.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams (sales, product development, medical affairs) to ensure cohesive marketing efforts.
  • Create and manage compelling marketing materials (brochures, presentations, website content) for healthcare professionals and patients (depending on regulations).

Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences, Marketing, Communications, or a related field (e.g., Pharmacy for specific roles).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to tailor messages to different audiences.
  • Proven ability to develop and execute successful marketing campaigns.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and digital marketing tools (e.g., CRM, marketing automation platforms).
